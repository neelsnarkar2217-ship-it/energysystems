Who We Are

Built on Trust.
Driven by Execution.

Energy Systems is a Pune-based industrial contractor with a proven track record across fabrication, erection, and solar EPC — delivered with precision, safety, and accountability.

Our Story

Site-Driven Since Day One

Energy Systems was founded with a clear purpose: to be a reliable, execution-focused partner for industrial utility and process projects. Starting from Pune, we built our reputation on the ground — at job sites, not in boardrooms.

Over five years, we have grown into a multi-disciplinary team capable of handling complex site conditions, diverse industries, and multi-location projects across PAN India. From mobilization to commissioning, every stage is managed with the same focus on quality and safety.

We are an Authorized Expert Partner of WAAREE Energies, extending our capabilities into solar EPC — residential, commercial, and industrial — with the same commitment to technical excellence.

Our Mission

To be a trusted partner in delivering Industrial Utility and Process Solutions with unwavering focus on customer satisfaction and execution excellence — through continual improvement in Quality, Performance, Safety, and On-Time Delivery.
